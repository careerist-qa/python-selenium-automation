Grilled Cheese and Ham

Ingredients

2 TBSP of Butter
2 slices of Sourdough Bread
2 slices of American Cheese
2 slices of Ham

Steps

1. Apply 1 TBSP of butter on one side of each piece of sourdough.
2. Pull out a skillet and put it on the stove and set heat to medium low.
3. Drop in one slice of bread butter side down.
4. Add 1 slice of cheese on top on the bread in the skillet.
5. Add both slices of ham onto the cheese.
6. Add the other slice of cheese on top of the ham.
7. Top it with the other slice of bread butter side up.
8. Flip after 5 minutes or until gold brown.
9. Take out of pan after 5 minutes or after both side are golden brown.
10. Slice diagonally and enjoy!
